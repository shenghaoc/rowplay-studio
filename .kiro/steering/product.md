# Product Steering

RowPlay Studio is the native macOS port of rowplay: Concept2 logbook analytics plus real-time workout replay for RowErg, SkiErg, and BikeErg athletes.

## Product Principles

- Replay-first: dashboard and analytics should lead users into understandable workout replay, not just static totals.
- Demo mode remains first-class so the app is explorable without a Concept2 token.
- BYOT privacy carries over from the web app. Personal Concept2 tokens must be stored locally and never logged.
- Native macOS should earn its keep with keyboard navigation, multiple-window readiness, settings, local files, offline cache, and eventual hardware transport.
- Bluetooth connectivity is deliberately later-phase work. It should feed the same live/replay model after the local app is stable.
